Ice cream world

Frozen is a food-dessert, which is a mass frozen in the process of continuous beating, which basically contains nutritious, flavoring, aromatic and emulsifying substances [1]. Ice cream is often also referred to as popsicle, obtained by simply freezing fruit and berry juices and mashed potatoes (fruit ice is most often made from an orange). Traditional types of ice cream are prepared on the basis of milk mixtures with certain ratios of milk protein and fat and / or on the basis of frozen juices, fruits and berries. They have a sweet taste due to the use of sugar as one of the main flavoring components, a preservative and a modifier of the rheological properties of the mixture at all stages of the ice cream production process. Agar-agar, gelatin, starch are used as an emulsifying component of industrially produced ice cream. There are ice cream recipes that use egg whites as an emulsifying component.

Milk is a nutrient fluid produced by the mammary glands of female mammals during lactation. The natural purpose of milk is to feed [1] offspring (including humans), which are not yet capable of digesting other food. Milk is now a component of many products used by humans, and its production has become a major industry. The technical regulation defines milk as a product of normal physiological secretion of the mammary glands of farm animals, obtained from one or more animals during lactation during one or more milking, without any additions to this product
